About The School

Gather and Stitch is a sewing school based in Louisville, Kentucky dedicated to learning and having FUN! Here's some of what we offer:

  1. Basic sewing skills classes
  2. Studio time to work on personal projects
  3. Craft nights
  4. Specialty classes including a wide variety of projects
  5. Other needlecraft classes, including crochet and knitting

If you have other project ideas or need a hand with a work in progress, I'd be happy to help with that as well. Send me an e-mail and we'll work something out!

About Melissa

My mom taught me to sew about 15 years ago and I'm SO thankful that she did. Sewing has brought me so much joy over the years and it's a such great feeling to produce something from nothing. The sense of accomplishment that comes from sewing is part of the reason I started this school. I want to bring that sense of accomplishment and confidence to a whole new generation of sewers. I think the art of sewing is a dying art and I want to do my part to keep the art alive. Please let me know if you have any questions about me or the school. I truly believe that ANYone can sew and I'm here to help you realize that!
